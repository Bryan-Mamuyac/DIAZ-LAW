## About This Project

This is the official website and administrative system of **Diaz Law Office**, a private law practice offering notarial services, civil and family law, business and corporate law, and criminal law consultations.

The platform provides a professional online presence for the office and a secure internal management system for the attorney and staff.

---

## Features

**Public Website**
- Professional landing page with services overview and call-to-action
- Online appointment booking with preferred date/time selection and issue type classification
- Contact form with direct messaging capability
- Attorney profile and areas of practice

**Admin Portal** *(private access only)*
- Appointment management with status tracking (Pending → Confirmed → Completed)
- Secretary notes and appointment date confirmation
- Messages inbox with unread indicators and direct email reply
- Financial records system with daily revenue and expense tracking
- Interactive analytics charts (daily line and bar graphs)
- Export to Excel (.xlsx) with separate Revenue and Expense sheets and smart filename generation
- Dark/Light mode toggle
- Mobile-responsive design
